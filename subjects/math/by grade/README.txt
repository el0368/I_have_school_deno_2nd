================================================================================
CURRICULUM PROGRESSION
================================================================================

KINDERGARTEN - GRADE 2: Building Foundations
---------------------------------------------
Focus Areas:
- Counting and number sense (1-100)
- Basic addition and subtraction (within 20, then 100, then 1,000)
- Introduction to place value
- Basic shapes and measurement
- Simple data representation
- Introduction to time and money

GRADE 3 - GRADE 5: Expanding Operations
----------------------------------------
Focus Areas:
- Multiplication and division mastery
- Fractions (understanding, operations, equivalence)
- Decimals (place value, operations)
- Area, perimeter, and volume
- Coordinate plane introduction
- Beginning algebraic thinking
- Data analysis with graphs

GRADE 6 - GRADE 8: Pre-Algebra & Algebra Foundations
-----------------------------------------------------
Focus Areas:
- Ratios, rates, and proportions
- Negative numbers and rational numbers
- Expressions, equations, and inequalities
- Linear equations and functions
- Systems of equations
- Exponents and scientific notation
- Geometry (transformations, Pythagorean theorem)
- Statistics and probability
- Introduction to irrational numbers

HIGH SCHOOL: Advanced Mathematics
----------------------------------

ALGEBRA 1:
- Linear equations and systems
- Functions and sequences
- Quadratic equations and functions
- Exponential growth and decay
- Polynomial operations

GEOMETRY:
- Transformations and congruence
- Similarity and trigonometry
- Circles and conic sections
- Solid geometry and volume
- Coordinate geometry
- Geometric constructions

ALGEBRA 2:
- Polynomial operations and graphs
- Complex numbers
- Rational functions
- Exponential and logarithmic functions
- Trigonometry
- Advanced modeling
