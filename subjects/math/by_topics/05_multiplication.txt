MULTIPLICATION
==============
Complete Topic Progression: Grade 3 → Algebra 2
Source: Khan Academy (exercises from by_grade files)

Multiplication starts with equal groups and times tables, then
extends through multi-digit, fractions, decimals, and polynomials.
Requires addition (02) and place value (04) foundations.
