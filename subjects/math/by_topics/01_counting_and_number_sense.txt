COUNTING AND NUMBER SENSE
=========================
Complete Topic Progression: Kindergarten → Grade 5
Source: Khan Academy (exercises from by_grade files)

This file traces NUMBER SENSE from first counting through rounding
and estimation. After Grade 5, number sense branches into negative
numbers (see 13_negative_numbers.txt) and algebra (see 15_algebra.txt).
