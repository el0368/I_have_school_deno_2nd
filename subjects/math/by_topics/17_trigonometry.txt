TRIGONOMETRY
============
Complete Topic Progression: Geometry → Precalculus → Calculus
Source: Khan Academy (exercises from by_grade files)

Trigonometry begins in High School Geometry with right-triangle
ratios and extends through unit circle, identities, and equations.
Requires solid geometry (09) and algebra (15) foundations.
