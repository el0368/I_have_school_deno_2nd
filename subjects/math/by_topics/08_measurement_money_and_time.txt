MEASUREMENT, MONEY, AND TIME
=============================
Complete Topic Progression: Kindergarten → Grade 5
Source: Khan Academy (exercises from by_grade files)

These practical math topics appear in K-5 and are prerequisites for
geometry and science applications. After Grade 5, measurement concepts
merge into geometry (see 09_geometry.txt) and unit conversion becomes
part of ratios (see 11_ratios_rates_proportions.txt).
