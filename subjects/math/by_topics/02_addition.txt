ADDITION
========
Complete Topic Progression: Kindergarten → Grade 5
Source: Khan Academy (exercises from by_grade files)

This file traces ADDITION from first sums through multi-digit
addition with decimals. After Grade 5, addition becomes part of
algebra (see 15_algebra.txt).
