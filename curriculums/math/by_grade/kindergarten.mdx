# Kindergarten Math Curriculum

Welcome to the Kindergarten Math learning path. Here you will find early math units broken down by topic.

## Unit 1: Counting and Place Value
- Count with small numbers
- Count in order
- Find 1 more or 1 less than a number
- Missing numbers
- Numbers to 100
- Count tens
- Count in pictures
- Count objects (1 and 2)
- Compare numbers of objects (1 and 2)
- Comparing numbers to 10
- Teen numbers

## Unit 2: Addition and Subtraction
- Add within 5
- Subtract within 5
- Making 5
- Making small numbers in different ways
- Make 10 (grids and number bonds)
- Make 10
- Add within 10
- Subtract within 10
- Addition word problems within 10
- Subtraction word problems within 10

## Unit 3: Measurement and Geometry
- Compare size
- Compare length
- Name shapes (1 and 2)
- Relative position
- Compare shapes
- Compose shapes
