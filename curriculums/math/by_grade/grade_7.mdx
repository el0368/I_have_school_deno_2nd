# 7th Grade Math Curriculum

Welcome to the 7th Grade Math learning path. Here you will find math units broken down by topic.

## Unit 1: Proportional Relationships
- Understanding constant of proportionality from graphs
- Constant of proportionality from equations
- Constant of proportionality from tables
- Identifying proportional relationships
- Solving proportions
- Proportion word problems

## Unit 2: Rates and Percentages
- Rates involving fractions
- Calculating percentages
- Percent of a number
- Percentage word problems
- Percentage increase and decrease
- Percent error
- Tax, tip, and discount problems

## Unit 3: Integers: Addition and Subtraction
- Operations with integer chips
- Adding negative numbers on the number line
- Subtracting negative numbers on the number line
- Adding and subtracting negative numbers
- Interpreting negative number statements
- Solving one-step equations with negatives

## Unit 4: Rational Numbers: Addition and Subtraction
- Absolute value to find distance
- Adding and subtracting negative fractions
- Adding and subtracting negative decimals
- Rational number word problems

## Unit 5: Negative Numbers: Multiplication and Division
- Multiplying negative numbers
- Dividing negative numbers
- Understanding signs of expressions
- Multiplication and division word problems with negatives

## Unit 6: Expressions, Equations, & Inequalities
- Combining like terms
- Combining like terms with negative coefficients
- Distributive property
- Writing expressions for word problems
- Solving two-step equations
- Solving two-step inequalities
- Multi-step equations
- Equation and inequality word problems

## Unit 7: Statistics and Probability
- Simple probability
- Experimental probability
- Comparing probabilities
- Making predictions with probability
- Probability models
- Sample spaces for compound events
- Making inferences from random samples
- Comparing distributions
- Statistical questions

## Unit 8: Scale Copies / Scale Drawings
- What are scaled copies?
- Creating scaled copies
- Scale factor
- Scale drawings
- Scale drawing word problems

## Unit 9: Geometry
- Radius and diameter
- Circumference of circles
- Area of circles
- Area of parts of circles
- Identifying supplementary, complementary, and vertical angles
- Finding missing angles
- Unknown angle problems using algebra
- Triangle side length rules
- Translations
- Reflections
- Rotations
- Dilations
