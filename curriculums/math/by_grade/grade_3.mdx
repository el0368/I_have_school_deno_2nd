# 3rd Grade Math Curriculum

Welcome to the 3rd Grade Math learning path. Here you will find math units broken down by topic.

## Unit 1: Intro to Multiplication
- Understanding equal groups as multiplication
- Multiplication with arrays
- Repeated addition and multiplication
- Multiplication on the number line
- Multiplication word problems

## Unit 2: 1-Digit Multiplication
- Multiplication facts (0-12)
- Multiplying by 0, 1, and 10
- Commutative property of multiplication
- Associative property of multiplication
- Distributive property
- Multiplication strategies
- 1-digit multiplication word problems

## Unit 3: Intro to Division
- Understanding division
- Division with equal groups
- Division on the number line
- Division word problems
- Relating multiplication and division

## Unit 4: More with Multiplication and Division
- Properties of multiplication
- Multiplying by tens
- Division facts
- Multiplication and division strategies
- Two-step word problems

## Unit 5: Addition, Subtraction, and Estimation
- Addition within 1,000
- Subtraction within 1,000
- Rounding to nearest 10 or 100
- Estimating sums and differences
- Two-step word problems

## Unit 6: Understanding Fractions
- What fractions mean
- Equal parts
- Fractions on a number line
- Unit fractions
- Fractions greater than 1
- Comparing fractions with same denominator
- Comparing fractions with same numerator

## Unit 7: Equivalent Fractions and Comparing Fractions
- Equivalent fractions
- Comparing fractions
- Ordering fractions
- Fraction word problems

## Unit 8: Area
- Counting unit squares to find area
- Area formulas
- Multiplying to find area
- Area of rectangles
- Decomposing figures to find area
- Area word problems

## Unit 9: Perimeter
- Understanding perimeter
- Finding perimeter
- Perimeter word problems
- Comparing area and perimeter

## Unit 10: Quadrilaterals and Plane Figures
- Classifying quadrilaterals
- Properties of shapes
- Identifying quadrilaterals
- Analyzing plane figures

## Unit 11: Measurement
- Measuring length
- Measuring mass
- Measuring volume (liquid)
- Telling time
- Elapsed time
- Measurement word problems

## Unit 12: Representing and Interpreting Data
- Picture graphs
- Bar graphs
- Line plots
- Interpreting data displays
- Data word problems

## Unit 13: Arithmetic Patterns and Problem Solving
- Number patterns
- Shape patterns
- Problem-solving strategies
- Multi-step word problems
