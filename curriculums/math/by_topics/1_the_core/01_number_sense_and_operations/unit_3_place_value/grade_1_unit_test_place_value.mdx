## Part 1 — Counting and Ordering *(6 points)*

**1.** Fill in the missing numbers. *(1 pt)*

$$44, \; 45, \; \_\_, \; \_\_, \; 48, \; \_\_, \; 50$$

**2.** Fill in the missing numbers. *(1 pt)*

$$97, \; 98, \; \_\_, \; 100, \; \_\_$$

**3.** Write the number **one hundred seven** as a numeral. *(1 pt)*

**4.** Order these numbers from **greatest to least**. *(1 pt)*

$$63, \; 18, \; 91, \; 45$$

**5.** What is one **more** than $99$? What is one **less** than $100$? *(1 pt each)*
