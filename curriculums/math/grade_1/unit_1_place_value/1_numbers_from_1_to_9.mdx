## âš–ï¸ Comparing Numbers: More, Less, or Equal?

We use three symbols when comparing:

| Symbol | Meaning | Example |
|:---:|---|---|
| $>$ | **greater than** (more) | $7 > 4$ |
| `\<` | **less than** (fewer) | $2 < 6$ |
| $=$ | **equal to** (same amount) | $5 = 5$ |

> **Memory trick:** The symbol's open mouth always faces the **bigger** number.
